Intelligent Deviation & Risk Management

Traditional deviation and risk management is slow, manual, and reactive. AHME can replace that with intelligent workflows that can perform risk assessments automatically or can surface root causes faster, auto-populate documentation, and reduce time-to-closure — paired with structured, auditable risk scoring that enables quality teams to act with speed, confidence, and full traceability.

GMP · CAPA · ICH Q9 · Risk · Decision Intelligence
Legacy System Modernization & AI Agent Integration

Your existing QMS, ERP, LIMS, and MES systems hold years of critical data — but they weren't built to work with AI. AHME specializes in bridging legacy infrastructure to modern AI agents through microSaaS connectors: read-only integrations that surface insights and guide decisions without ever writing directly to validated systems. Human approval stays in the loop. Compliance stays intact.

AI Agents · MicroSaaS · Legacy Bridge · 21 CFR Part 11 · Parallel AI Layer
Automated Trending & Quality Intelligence

QMR preparation, Annual Reports, KPI dashboards, trend detection, and executive-ready visualizations — built to run automatically. Replace hours of manual data compilation with real-time intelligence that drives proactive quality management.

QMR · Annual Reports · KPIs · Trend Analysis
Smart Troubleshooting & Knowledge Capture

When your most experienced people walk out the door, their knowledge shouldn't go with them. AHME builds intelligent knowledge systems that capture institutional expertise, guide troubleshooting in real time, and make that knowledge accessible to every team member.

Knowledge Mgmt · Guided Workflows

Founder's Perspective

Built from
real experience

AHME was born in the field — in GMP environments where teams carried extraordinary responsibility, often with tools that hadn't evolved in a decade. Where audit pressure was constant, institutional knowledge lived in the heads of a few key people, and the gap between what was needed and what existed was felt every single day.

That gap sparked a question: What if the systems could keep up with the people? What if intelligence could be designed for the realities of regulated work — not generic enterprise software retrofitted to fit?

AHME is the answer. Purpose-built for regulated industries, grounded in practitioner experience, and driven by the conviction that the best technology disappears — you simply feel the results.
